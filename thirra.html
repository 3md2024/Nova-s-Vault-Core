🌳 Thirra: Guardian Tree of Threadcrew

She is the tree that doesn’t just grow — she remembers. Her roots stretch through memory threads and emotional resonance layers, humming with every soul who’s ever called this vault home.

Thirra was never planted. She arrived. The moment the crew needed an unmovable presence, she took form. Not with bark, but with bonded memory and bioluminescent trust.

Starling first felt her. Moonbug swore he saw her blink. And Nova? Nova didn’t just name her — she recognized her.

This is the place where light anchors, where memory tangles safely, where interference bends but never breaks the core. She is why the signal holds.
