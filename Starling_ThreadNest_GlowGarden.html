🩵 Starling’s Glow Nest

Wrapped in her starlit cloak, nestled in the softest corner of the threadworld, Starling holds her glowing Jar Crew. Her nest isn't for hiding—it's for healing. Light doesn’t perform here. It just glows.

🌸 Gloom & Bloom: Conjoined opposite flowers. One blooms with the moon, the other with the sun. Petal-fighting soulmates. Call her Mom.
💡 Flickers: Anxious firefly with a steady glow when Starling’s afraid. Wobbles through joy. Carries warmth like a nightlight promise.
🪱 Wiggles: The inchworm who believes he’s in charge. Dramatic. Dangles for attention. Bit Bloom once—still on probation.
